Poetry

Falling in Love
in February

Written by Karylle Gumba & Kate Antonette Olivar
As I gaze upon the window
Rainbow of colors show in the sky
Seems new to my heart
But just another feeling flitting in my time

Walking through the streets
A wind tiptoed in my feet
I could not breathe, my heart skipped a beat
And just like that, my world became sweet

I never planned to feel this way,
It came so fast, I had no say;
One fleeting look, one gentle smile,
And everything feels surreal in a while

The day pass by, the air felt light,
Your presence lingered in my mind;
No words were shared, no hands held tight,
Yet love was born in plain daylight.

If this is all February gives
A gentle moment, the way love lives
Then I will hold this truth in sight
Some hearts just know love at first sight

And though this love began so small,
It whispers truths that touch us all;
Not always grand, not always loud,
Yet love can lift, can move, and stay unbowed

And love, is not just a tender spark,
It lights the dark, it warms the nights
It whispers softly, It draws us near,
A daily presence, we always feel.

About this Poem

A collaborative love poem capturing the quiet, unexpected magic of falling in love — tender moments that bloom in ordinary days, stitching two hearts together in the gentlest of ways.

Classroom Rules

Our Classroom, Our Community

Thoughtfully crafted rules that build a safe, respectful, and joy-filled learning environment where every student feels seen, valued, and empowered to grow.

📋
01
Be Present
Give your full attention to learning and to one another. Put devices away and engage with genuine curiosity.
02
Be Kind
Treat every person with respect and empathy, always — inside and outside the classroom walls.
03
Be Curious
Ask questions, explore ideas, and never stop wondering. Every question is worth asking.
04
Be Responsible
Own your actions, your words, and your work. Integrity matters in everything you do.
05
Be Courageous
Raise your hand, share your thoughts, make mistakes — and grow from every single one of them.
06
Be a Team
Celebrate each other's wins and lift each other up. We grow stronger together as a community.
